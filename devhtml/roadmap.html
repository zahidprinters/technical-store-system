Phase 1: Foundation (COMPLETED)

Store Settings

Global configuration with 46 fields across 7 tabs. ERPNext integration toggle and demo data management.

Store UOM

Units of measure with 27+ predefined units and conversion factors. Validation against invalid conversions.

Store Item Group

Hierarchical tree structure with unlimited nesting. Real-time statistics and configuration inheritance.

Store Location

6-level warehouse hierarchy with auto-generation. Intelligent naming patterns and cascading dropdowns.

Store Item

Complete item master with 64 fields. Multi-UOM support and flexible tracking options (Serial/Batch/None).

Tracking Tables

Serial and batch number child tables for individual item and lot tracking with expiry management.
